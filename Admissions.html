Application Process

Step 1: Create an account
Step 2: Complete your application
Step 3: Upload required documents
Step 4: Submit & pay application fee
Step 5: Await confirmation

Requirements

Undergraduate: KCSE Certificate, ID or Passport, Recommendation Letters.
Postgraduate: Degree Certificate, Transcripts, 2 References.
International: Passport, English Proficiency Proof, Equivalency Letter.

Fees & Payment Info

All tuition fees are payable per semester. You can pay via bank transfer, mobile money, or online card payments.

Financial Aid & Scholarships

We offer need-based scholarships and merit-based aid. Apply early to maximize your chances of receiving support.

Application Deadlines

Undergraduate: August 15
Postgraduate: September 30
International: July 15
