University Overview

Kabarak University is a leading Christian institution of higher learning committed to academic excellence, holistic development, and servant leadership.

Vision, Mission & Core Values

Vision

To be a centre of academic excellence founded on Biblical Christian values.

Mission

To provide holistic quality education based on research, practical skills, and Biblical Christian values.

Core Values

Integrity, Innovation, Stewardship, Excellence, Servanthood.

History & Founding Story

Founded in 2002 by the second President of Kenya, H.E. Daniel T. arap Moi, Kabarak University was envisioned as a Christian-based centre of academic excellence.
