University Overview

Mc Legendry University is a premier Christian institution dedicated to nurturing intellectual excellence, moral integrity, and servant leadership. Our mission is to empower students with the knowledge, values, and skills to become transformative leaders in society. With a commitment to innovation, character development, and global impact, we provide a holistic learning environment that blends academic rigor with spiritual growth.

Located in a serene and inspiring setting, Mc Legendry University offers a wide range of accredited undergraduate and postgraduate programs, state-of-the-art facilities, and a vibrant campus life. Our graduates are distinguished by their competence, compassion, and commitment to making a difference in the world.

History & Founding Story

Mc Legendry University was established in 2002 through the visionary leadership of Dr. Ababu Emmanuel Oturi. His dream was to create a Christian-based institution that would blend academic excellence with strong moral values and servant leadership.

From humble beginnings, the university has grown into a respected center of higher education, attracting students from across the country and beyond. The founding philosophy was rooted in nurturing holistic individuals—intellectually empowered, spiritually grounded, and ethically responsible.

Today, Mc Legendry University stands as a beacon of transformative education, committed to producing graduates who not only excel in their fields but also uphold integrity, faith, and service to humanity.

Accreditation & Affiliations

Mc Legendry University is a fully accredited institution recognized by the Commission for University Education (CUE), ensuring compliance with national and global academic standards.

  • Commission for University Education (CUE): Officially certified to operate as a degree-awarding institution in Kenya.
  • International Association of Universities (IAU): A proud member connecting us to a global academic community.
  • Africa Network for Internationalization of Education (ANIE): Strengthening our cross-border academic partnerships.
  • Google Developer Student Clubs (GDSC): Supported partnership enhancing student tech innovation.
  • Partnerships with Global Institutions: We collaborate with universities in the UK, USA, South Korea, and Canada for research, exchange, and dual-degree programs.

These affiliations open doors for students to participate in international exchange programs, collaborative research, and global conferences—preparing them for success in a competitive global market.
