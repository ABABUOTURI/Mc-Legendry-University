About Us

THE UNIVERSITY.



Mc Legendry University is a private Chartered Institution of higher learning that provides holistic,quality education,research and outreach activitiesaimed at producing graduates who are fully equiped for the service of God and humanity. Founded in the year 2002 by Prof Emmanuel Ababu Oturi,as a result of his visionary idea, set up a Christian University that would meet the demand for higher education in Kenya and the Globe and offer quality education based on strong principles. The Institution was granted Letter of interim Authority in October 2002 opening doors for its first batch of students in the year 2002.Later, in the year 2008,the University was awarded Charter by the third President of the Republic of Kenya,His Excellency Hon.Mwai Kibaki, making it fully-fledged accredited University.In November 2017 Mc Legendry University acquired ISO 9005:2015 certification.
