Welcome Note!!

Welcome to the Mc Legendry University Library, a client-focused and a world-class learning resource center.

Our library plays an important role in increasing accessibility and dissemination of research. As knowledge and information intermediaries, we endeavor to collect, organize, and store relevant information materials both in print and electronic media.

Mc Legendry University has two Libraries at the Main Campus, Nairobi Campus, and Rwanda Campus respectively. In the two Libraries, we have created the infrastructure to deliver services in the digital library environment.

Our Commitment.

Being the nerve center of the University, we provide quality services by acquiring relevant, authoritative, up-to-date materials and promoting quick access and delivery of Information communication technology.

Classification and arrangement of books.

The Library uses the Library of Congress Classification System to organize its collection. Resources are cataloged using the Anglo-American Cataloging Rules (AACR2).
